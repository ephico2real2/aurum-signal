## §4 Industry research findings

### Pattern F — Chop-session breakout (Apr 6 Asian)

**Canonical pattern** (verbatim, [LiteFinance ORB Strategy](https://www.litefinance.org/blog/for-beginners/trading-strategies/opening-range-breakout-strategy/)):
> "Opening Range Breakout (ORB) is a classic intraday momentum strategy... traders define an 'opening range' as the high and low formed during the first few minutes (typically 5–60 minutes) after the market opens, then wait for the price to break decisively above the range high (bullish breakout) or below the range low (bearish breakout)."

Applied to FORGE: Asian session opens at ~00:00 UTC. The first 30 min establishes the Asian box. Break of box-low confirmed by body close = SELL signal. Apr 6 broke box-low at ~01:30 with body close 4642 (box-low ~4647).

**Verbatim** ([ICT London Killzone / Arongroups](https://arongroups.co/forex-articles/ict-london-killzone-time/)):
> "Liquidity grabs are a defining feature of the ICT London killzone where price frequently targets stops above the Asian high or below the Asian low..."

Apr 6 01:30 swept the Asian low — classic stop hunt. The fade entry (re-entry into the range after sweep) is a **counter-trend bounce** captured by ASIA_CAPITULATION_BUY (Run 3 took this at 01:47 @ 4608). The CASCADE-down entry (during the sweep, riding the move) is what's missing.

### Pattern G — Range-day SELL avoidance (Apr 6 / Apr 7)

**Canonical caution** ([ACY: Asian Session Secrets](https://acy.com/en/market-news/education/market-education-asian-session-usdjpy-volatility-trading-strategy-j-o-20250818-092018/)):
> "Asian Session Secrets: How Smart Money Uses Accumulation & Fake Breakouts... False breakouts occur above or below the range, often during the late Asian session (1:00 AM–3:00 AM EST), with these sweeps clearing retail stops..."

Strong support for Operator's rule: **chop SELL composites must filter for "real breakdown, not sweep"**. The atom: require **2 consecutive M5 closes below box-low** (not just one), AND M15-ADX > 20.

### Pattern H — Sustained chop rally without macro alignment (Apr 6 11:00 / Apr 7 PM)

[Mind Math Money VWAP](https://www.mindmathmoney.com/articles/vwap-trading-strategy-the-ultimate-guide-to-volume-weighted-average-price-in-tradingview-2025):
> "VWAP acts as the day's volume-weighted fair value... price-above-VWAP signals institutional buy-side flow even when broader trend filters are noisy."

Apr 6 11:00 (price 4685, VWAP gap +43) and Apr 7 16:00+ (consistent +VWAP) both showed institutional buy-side flow without h1 confirmation. A **VWAP-anchor BUY composite** (Composite E) catches these.

Already documented in `FORGE_CASE_STUDY_2026_03_31_to_04_08.md` §4b. Patterns from [LuxAlgo Order Flow VWAP Deviation](https://www.luxalgo.com/library/indicator/order-flow-vwap-deviation/):
